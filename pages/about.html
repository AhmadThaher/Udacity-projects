About Us

Space X Gaming Cafe was established in 2024 to provide a premium gaming experience for gamers of all ages. Our mission is to create a community where gamers can come together to enjoy the latest games and technology in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

Our facilities include high-end gaming PCs, the latest PlayStation 5 consoles, and a fully immersive VR room. Whether you're a casual gamer or a competitive player, Space X Gaming Cafe has something for everyone.
